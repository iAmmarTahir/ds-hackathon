Below is a list of the changes included in the SC-Spring-2024.6 release of Sound Transit GTFS data.

Schedule Changes
* disruption schedule for the 1 Line starting May 12. Disruption will continue until June 23. The June 21-23 disruption with a bus bridge will be included in the next GTFS update.

Route Changes
* none

Stop Changes
* update Sounder stops to label stops by track number rather than by direction of travel (since tracks may be used in either direction)
* add blank level_id field to support future use of pathways

Other Changes
